Who are we?

Rareloop is a web development company based in Southampton with the intentions of bringing your needs to life through the use of the most advanced technologies available.

Fancy a Visit?

We're located in the center of Southampton at the Rareloop offices. Arrange a time to visit & make use of the devices we have available, from smartphones to tablets.

Support Us

Anyone can participate and help the developer community of Southampton by lending their devices to improve our collection. Contributions are always welcome.

Contact Form

From booking a device to donating a new device, use the form below to handle any queries you may have. Don't forget to name the device you would like to use for testing along with the date & time you would prefer.
